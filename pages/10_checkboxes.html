Shift-clicking checkboxes

Neat little trick to allow you to shift-click to check every box between a first box and the one that gets shift-clicked.

Decidedly confusing little piece of coding. Lots of balls to keep in the air as it were.

This is an inbox layout.

Check one item

Hold down your Shift key

Check a lower item

Everything in between should also be set to checked

Try do it without any libraries

Just regular JavaScript

Good Luck!

Oh hey and it works backwards (bottom-to-top) as well.
